Calf Muesli 18%

Premium textured feed combining the benefits of whole grains with high-quality protein sources for exceptional calf nutrition and palatability.

The Perfect Blend of Nutrition and Palatability

Our Calf Muesli 18% offers a unique approach to calf feeding, combining the natural appeal of whole grains with carefully selected protein sources. This textured feed encourages natural feeding behaviors while delivering optimal nutrition for growing calves.

The muesli format provides variety in texture and taste, making it highly palatable for young animals while ensuring they receive balanced nutrition for healthy growth and development.

Key Features

  • Textured Muesli Format: Encourages natural feeding behaviors
  • 18% Quality Protein: Supports steady growth and development
  • Whole Grain Goodness: Includes premium rolled grains
  • Highly Palatable: Excellent feed conversion rates
  • Digestive Health: Supports rumen development
  • Visual Appeal: Attractive mix calves love to eat

Key Feed Components

  • Premium rolled barley and wheat
  • High-quality protein meals
  • Calcium carbonate for bone development
  • Essential vitamin and mineral premix
  • Molasses for palatability and dust control
  • Salt for electrolyte balance

Feeding Recommendations

Introduction Period: Gradually introduce over 7-10 days, mixing with current feed.

Daily Feeding Rate: 1.8-3.5 kg per head per day, depending on calf age and weight.

Suitable Age: Ideal for calves from 8 weeks to 12 months of age.

Feeding Method: Can be fed in troughs or feeders, ensure adequate feeding space.

Water Access: Always provide unlimited access to clean, fresh water.

25kg bags for convenience and bulk options for larger farming operations
