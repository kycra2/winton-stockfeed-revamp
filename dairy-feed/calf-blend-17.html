Calf Blend 17%

Premium pelleted blend designed for growing calves aged 3-12 months, providing optimal nutrition for healthy development and growth.

Complete Nutrition for Growing Calves

Our Calf Blend 17% is expertly formulated to support the critical growth phase of young calves. This premium pelleted feed provides the perfect balance of protein, energy, vitamins, and minerals essential for healthy bone development, muscle growth, and immune system support.

Manufactured using high-quality ingredients and advanced pelleting technology, this feed ensures excellent palatability and digestibility for optimal calf performance.

Key Benefits

  • 17% Protein Content: Optimal protein level for steady growth
  • Balanced Energy: Provides sustained energy for active calves
  • Enhanced Digestibility: Pelleted form improves nutrient absorption
  • Vitamin & Mineral Fortified: Complete nutritional support
  • Palatable Formula: Encourages consistent feed intake

Nutritional Information (Dry Matter Basis)

Crude Protein 17.0% min
Metabolisable Energy 12.5 MJ/kg DM
Crude Fat 3.5% min
Crude Fibre 8.0% max
Calcium 0.8% min
Phosphorus 0.5% min
Salt 0.7% max
Vitamin E 40 IU/kg min

Feeding Recommendations

Introduction: Gradually introduce over 7-10 days, mixing with current feed.

Feeding Rate: 1.5-3.0 kg per head per day, depending on calf size and growth targets.

Age Range: Suitable for calves 3-12 months of age.

Water: Ensure constant access to clean, fresh water.

Storage: Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.
