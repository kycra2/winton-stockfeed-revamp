Eco Calf Finisher

Sustainable, cost-effective finishing feed designed for environmentally conscious farming while maintaining excellent calf growth performance.

Sustainable Nutrition for Modern Farming

Our Eco Calf Finisher represents the future of sustainable livestock nutrition. Formulated using locally-sourced ingredients and environmentally responsible practices, this feed delivers exceptional performance while reducing your farm's environmental footprint.

Designed for the finishing phase of calf development, this feed provides optimal nutrition for achieving target weights efficiently and economically.

Key Benefits

  • Sustainable Formulation: Environmentally responsible ingredient sourcing
  • Cost-Effective: Excellent value without compromising quality
  • Complete Nutrition: Balanced for optimal finishing performance
  • Local Sourcing: Supporting New Zealand agriculture
  • Reduced Carbon Footprint: Minimized transport distances
  • Proven Results: Consistent weight gains and feed conversion

Our Sustainability Commitment

At Winton Stock Feed, we believe in responsible farming practices that protect our environment for future generations. Our Eco Calf Finisher is formulated with this commitment in mind:

  • Local ingredient sourcing to reduce transportation emissions
  • Utilization of agricultural by-products to minimize waste
  • Efficient feed conversion to reduce overall resource consumption
  • Supporting sustainable farming practices across New Zealand

Feeding Recommendations

Introduction: Gradually introduce over 10-14 days for optimal rumen adaptation.

Feeding Rate: 3.0-5.0 kg per head per day during finishing phase (6+ months).

Target Weight: Designed for finishing calves to target sale weights.

Supplementation: Can be fed alongside quality pasture or hay.

Water Requirements: Ensure constant access to clean, fresh water.
