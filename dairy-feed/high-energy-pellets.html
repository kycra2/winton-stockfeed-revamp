High Energy Pellets

Premium energy-dense pellets specifically formulated for high-producing dairy cows requiring additional energy support for peak milk production.

Maximum Energy for Peak Performance

Our High Energy Pellets are engineered for dairy cows in peak lactation or high-stress periods requiring concentrated energy support. These premium pellets deliver maximum metabolizable energy in a highly digestible format, supporting milk production while maintaining body condition.

Formulated with high-quality energy sources and essential nutrients, these pellets are perfect for supplementing pasture-based systems or enhancing TMR diets during critical production periods.

Key Benefits

  • High Energy Density: Maximum ME per kg for efficient nutrition
  • Rapid Absorption: Quick energy release for immediate support
  • Superior Palatability: Excellent intake and feed conversion
  • Consistent Quality: Reliable energy delivery in every pellet
  • Flexible Feeding: Easy to incorporate into any feeding system
  • Body Condition Support: Helps maintain optimal body score

Why Choose High Energy Pellets?

During peak lactation, cows face significant energy demands that can exceed what they consume through normal feeding. Our High Energy Pellets bridge this gap by providing concentrated nutrition that supports:

  • Enhanced milk production and milk quality
  • Improved feed efficiency and conversion rates
  • Better reproduction performance and fertility
  • Reduced metabolic stress during transition periods
  • Optimal body condition maintenance

Feeding Guidelines

Introduction: Gradually introduce over 5-7 days to prevent digestive upset.

Feeding Rate: 2-6 kg per cow per day, depending on production level and body condition.

Timing: Best fed during milking or as a TMR component.

Target Cows: High-producing cows, fresh cows, or cows in poor body condition.

Monitoring: Regular body condition scoring recommended to optimize feeding rates.

Professional Advice: Consult with a nutritionist for farm-specific recommendations.

Usage Recommendations

Peak Lactation Support: Provide extra energy when cows are producing at their highest levels.

Transition Period: Support cows through the challenging fresh cow period.

Body Condition Recovery: Help thin cows regain optimal body condition score.

Pasture Supplementation: Bridge energy gaps during poor pasture conditions.

Winter Feeding: Provide additional energy during cold weather stress.
