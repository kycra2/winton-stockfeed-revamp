Calf Grower 20%

High-protein pelleted feed specifically formulated for rapid calf growth and development during the critical early growth phase.

Maximum Growth Potential

Our Calf Grower 20% represents the pinnacle of calf nutrition science. With an elevated 20% protein content, this premium feed is designed for farmers who demand the very best growth rates and health outcomes for their young stock.

Formulated with high-quality protein sources and essential nutrients, this feed supports rapid lean muscle development while maintaining optimal bone growth and immune function.

Key Benefits

  • 20% High-Quality Protein: Maximum growth potential
  • Optimized Amino Acid Profile: Enhanced muscle development
  • Superior Digestibility: Advanced pelleting for better nutrient utilization
  • Complete Vitamin Package: Supports immune system and overall health
  • Mineral Fortified: Promotes strong bone and skeletal development
  • Consistent Quality: Reliable nutrition in every batch

Feeding Recommendations

Introduction: Gradually introduce over 10-14 days to allow rumen adaptation.

Feeding Rate: 2.0-4.0 kg per head per day, adjusted based on calf weight and target growth rates.

Target Age: Ideal for calves 2-8 months seeking maximum growth.

Fresh Water: Unlimited access to clean water is essential.

Monitoring: Regular weight checks recommended to optimize feeding rates.
